            Executioner
            Enemy Type Neither Beast nor Kin
            Health 343 ~ 409
            Echoes Tempering Blood Gemstone, Blood Vial (3-4), Pungent Blood Cocktail
            340 ~ 4379 Blood Echoes
            Weak Bolt, Blunt, Arcane
            Strong Physical, Thrust & Fire


            Executioner is an Enemy in Bloodborne.

            Executioner Information

            Executioners are very broad enemies carrying large axes. They are very difficult at base level due to their large amount of health, swift attacks and high damage output.

            Strategy

            • The Hunter Blunderbuss is an effective weapon for stunning these foes. Due to the very fast attack speed of this enemy, is best to fire the weapon as soon as the attack animation begins.
            • Due to its long reach and power, the Hunter Axe is an effective weapon against executioners. The Axe can easily stagger them during their guarding stance. They will also be flung back by a charged R2 attack, opening them up for a killing blow.
            • Any heavy weaponry, like Ludwig's Holy Blade, is advisable to carry into battle against one. When they take a protective stance, a crushing blow will have them reel from the attack, and open them to an additional, usually fatal strike.
            • If you can sneak up behind them without catching their attention, they are very susceptible to charged attacks and visceral hits.

            Notes & Trivia

            • Has a high chance of dropping a tempering blood gemstone, which can be used for weapon upgrades. Helpful if you want to farm gems early in the game.
            • Executioners wear a plated helmet with two round openings for their eyes.
            • At the height of their navel, their armour depicts the face of a lady.
            • Looking up their cape reveals greasy dreadlocks, but these could just as well be tentacles.
            • The plates on their shoulders have an embossment on them that from the right angle looks like a foot with toes and an eye on it. The metal foot is characteristic of Smough (Dark Souls) but also the Looking Glass Knight (Dark Souls 2). Amygdala happens to have feet with eyes on them. It could be that Amygdala and the Executioners are connected somehow.
            • Similar to the Ya'hargul Set, the attire worn is one of the few rare examples of traditional armor in the game/

                                                    So this is a little far-fetched, but seeing as it's a Fromsoft game I figured I'd share my theory. We know the Souls games have a penchant for adding grotesque, thicc, strong women to their games, starting with the Butchers in Dark Souls 1 and continuing with NPCs for the rest of the series. After looking at the detailed pictures of the Executioners on this page, I think a strong argument exists in favor of the Executioners being female (or at least having womanly attributes). First, as the wiki notes, they have long dreadlocked black hair. Men, of course, can also have long hair, so this isn't evidence in itself. In the same vein, the "overweight" appearance of the armor makes the breastplate look like just that—breasts. However, what really made me think about this was the cameo on the Executioners' stomachs. Look at the above picture of the staggered Executioner in Central Yharnam. At first glance, it looks like a portrait of a hooded woman surrounded by a circular frame. However, given the recurring theme of pregnancy and childbirth in Bloodborne, another possible interpretation exists. Picture someone wearing a hoodie with a tight hood. It almost looks like they're being born, doesn't it? With their face poking out of the hoodie-womb? Look at the picture of the Executioner again. Notice the shape of the portrait woman's hood: how it starts at a point, opens up, and closes loosely at the bottom. Notice the effect of the "folds" surrounding the hood. TL;DR, the Executioner armor looks like a pregnant woman giving birth.
