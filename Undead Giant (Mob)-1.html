            Undead Giant
            Enemy Type Not Beast Nor Kin
            Health ??
            Drops Warm Blood Gems, frequently drops adept gems.
            Weak  
            Strong ??
            Locations Chalice Dungeons

            Undead Giant is an enemy in Bloodborne. It may also be encountered as a boss in Chalice Dungeons.

            Undead Giant Information

            • This enemy has four variations, all variations may appear as bosses in labyrinths.
            • One with two sickles instead of hands (standard melee giant)
            • One with a cannon and an axe (standard ranged giant)
            • One with a flaming sickle (standard melee giant)
            • One armed with a club, sickle and flails on his/her back (Boss only giant)

             

            Strategy

            • All giants may spawn with a bloated hemorrhoid on their body, destroying the hemorrhoid deals massive dmg.
            • All giants start using a roaring smash to the ground when their health gets low, this produces a medium sized shockwave.
            • All giants are prone to use wide swing attacks when players are behind them.
            • All giants can use a fast but powerful kick.
            • Cannon giant has a very long range, good accuracy and may fire several times in a row.
            • Twin Sickles giant alternates between wide swings and long range thrusts and may combo up to 4 times.
            • The flail giants (club and sickle) start using the flails on their back when their health gets low, this makes their attacks much more damaging and much harder to dodge.
