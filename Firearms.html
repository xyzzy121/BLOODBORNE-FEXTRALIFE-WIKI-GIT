            Equipment / Weapons
              +

            Firearms are Weapons in Bloodborne that are used in the left hand and are employed in beast hunting. They fire projectiles at the enemy, doing damage and various other effects. They can also be used to parry enemies.

                                                                    Since I can't edit the page I'll just add this here:
                                                                    A bit of interesting trivia, not much, but you can perform a rolling attack (or rollshot) if you shoot and projectile firearm (piston, canon, blunderbuss etc.) after a roll. Your character will be recovering from the roll animation and will stand on one knee to shoot the weapon. This does not add any special properties to the attack and can only be performed when rolling meaning that you need to be unlocked, so isn't too practical.

                                                                    Another bit of trivia is that you can use the Monocular and still shoot with your gun, you even get a reticle, turning this into a third person shooter almost. This is useful if you want to hit a specific target like aggro a moving target or Patches in the nightmare.
