            Tools & Calculators
              +

            Summon Range Calculator is a simple tool that allows you to determine the possible range of available summons in Bloodborne.

            Online Play has a summoning component that is regulated by a simple average of the Level of the host and the guests. Please be warned that if you are using a Password, you can match with ANY level, as of Patch 1.04

            Instructions: Insert the level of your character and press "Calculate".






            • Please note the password does not affect pvp or invasions.

                              If you want to coop with others seriously check out the subreddit r/huntersbell, it's been mentioned but I'll do it again just to get it across.
                              Also get the SRRC (short ritual root chalice, in Hintertomb dungeons or you can buy for 10 Insight from the Insight Dream bath after clearing Central Pthurmeru dungeon) and use the co-op match option (option #2) to help other people in dungeons.
                              Also note that as a Beckoner or Cooperator, you can ring your bell at the start of an area/layer and leave it going as you scamper around and the game can still match you.
                              As long as you don't die, your bell is still going, which can be useful especially in dungeons if Beckoning with low Insight - you can finish a layer and progress to the next and summon on that same bell, even if you have already summoned on the prior level (or if your coop dies, you don't have to rering, they can come back in as long as they ring their small bell again from the same dungeon layer
