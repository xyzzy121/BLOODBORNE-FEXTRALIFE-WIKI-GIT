            Huntsmen
            Enemy Type Not Beast, Nor Kin
            Health 70 ~ 518
            Drops Blood Vials, Quicksilver Bullets, Pungent Blood Cocktail, Oil Urn, Bone Marrow Ash, 48 ~ 695 Blood Echoes
            Weak Nothing
            Strong Nothing
            Locations Central Yharnam, Healing Church Workshop, Forbidden Woods, Yahar'gul, Cathedral Ward, Hemwick Charnel Lane
            NG+? Yes

            Huntsmen in Bloodborne are human enemies hunting threats to Yharnam. Due to the infestation of the scourge, this is what the mob that has risen for the beast hunt have become. The fever-like urge to hunt remains, but they themselves have already been infested with the scourge of the beast. Unaware of this fact, they continue to search for prey to hunt and kill. Perhaps through their murky eyes, humans are seen as the true beasts.

            Hunter Mob Information


            This is possibly the most common mob you will encounter in the game, and they are greatly varied. The variations all share some likeness, but require attention in terms of which strategy to adopt. The huntsmen are mostly low to mid-level enemies; in large groups they can still be challenging, however, particularly the molotov and rifle variants.

            Strategy

             

            • The shield-bearing variant will be able to block one or two hits before becoming staggered and exposed.
            • It is important to consider one's distance between oneself and the rifle variant: the closer the shooter, the more damage he inflicts.
            • Pitchfork variants have long and deceiving range, it is recommended to sidestep when dodging their swing
            • Due to being slow, these enemies can easily be parried.
            • They will usually travel in horde with 1 or 2 rifle variant. It's recommended to take these out first before taking the rest out
