            Old Hunters are friendly NPCs that may be summoned for co-operation using the Old Hunter Bell. This page lists all available hunters and their summon locations. You will not obtain Vermin for co-op with NPCs, so if you are after ranks for The League you should instead be summoned as a helper into another player's world.

            These summon signs glow in an indigo or red hue like these:

            Old Hunters (sorted by location)

            Zone Hunter Name & Details

            Central Yharnam and Old Yharnam            		 Old Hunter Vileblood Hunter  Alfred: Summon sign glows a red hue if you chose to cooperate with him in Cathedral Ward. His summon sign is located at the entrance to the house that leads to the Cleric Beast.

            His summon sign is located on the right side of the stairs just before Blood-Starved Beast. However, in order for his sign to be there you must not have killed Vicar Amelia.

            He wields the Kirkhammer with Ludwig's rifle while wearing the Executioner attire.

            Cathedral Ward

            Old Hunter Henriet: Summon sign glows a red hue.

            Her summon sign is located in a corner near the base of the ladder leading to numbing mists.

            She wields Kirkhammer with the Repeating Pistol while wearing the Hunter attire with cape.

            Upper Cathedral Ward and Brygenwerth

            Old Hunter Mensis Scholar Damian: Summon sign glows a red hue.

            His summon sign is located at the bottom of the elevator leading to Ebrietas.

            His summon sign is located at the upper floor of Byrgenwerth near the locked door leading to Rom. Summoning him here cancels out (Henryk/Younger Madaras Twin).

            His summon sign will NOT be available if you have summoned Henryk and/or Younger Madaras Twin.

            His summon sign is located at ? Great Isz Chalice layer 3?

            He wields Threaded Cane with Loch Shield while wearing the Student attire with Cage of Mensis. In addition, he uses A Call Beyond, Choir bell, and Black Sky Eye Hunter Tools.


            Lower Pthumeru Chalice            		 Old Hunter Madman Wallar: Summon sign glows a indigo hue.

            His summon sign is located on the right hand side of the square plaza in Layer 3.

            He wields the Amygdalan Arm with Ludwig's Rifle while wearing the Madman attire.

            He is also located on layer 4 with a blue summon sign in the side section between the main door and the boss door after a room full of enemies.
            Prospector Olek: Summon sign glows a red hue.

            His summon sign on layer 3 is located right before the Rom boss fight on the left side before going down the stairs.

            Extra Note: Can summon both Waller and Olek on layer 3 for Rom boss fight.

            Olek is in layer 3 of first chalice. right hand side after lamp glows red. doesnt need impurity rune

            His summon sign on layer 4 red is located after entering the main layer behind a door you can only open from the other side. You'll have to go around and jump down into the room.


            Defiled Chalice            		 Old Hunter Queen Killer: Summon sign glows a red hue.

            His summon sign is located before the boss gate on layer 2 and 3 of the Defiled Chalice.

            Summoning Queen Killer on layer 2 cancels out Olek.

            Queen Killer is naked wielding the Logarius wheel and the Cannon.
            Old Hunter Madman Waller: Summon sign glows a indigo hue. Summoning him on layer 3 won't cancel out Queen Killer.

            He wields the Amygdalan Arm with Ludwig's Rifle while wearing the Madman attire.

            Old Hunter Olek: Summon sign glows a red hue.

            His summon sign is located near the lamp on your left hand side.

            Summoning Olek on layer 2 will cancel out Queen Killer.

            Impurity Rune is not needed to summon these Hunters

            Forbidden Woods            		 Confederate Younger Madaras Twin: Summon sign glows a red hue. Need impurity rune equipped to find it.

            His summon sign is located in Forbidden Woods near the Clockwise Metamorphosis rune.

            His summon sign is located in Byrgenwerth right after lamp in between 2 trees.

            He wield the Hunter's Axe and Hunter's Blunderbuss.
            Byrgenwerth

            Old Hunter Henryk: Summon sign glows a red hue.

            His summon sign is located in Forbidden Woods, before theShadow of Yharnam fight. 

            Also available at Byrgenwerth on the second floor near the hostile choir hunter.

            In order for his summon sign to appear you must have succesfully killed him with Eileen. If Eileen dies fighting him and you kill him, his summon signs in Forbidden Woods and Byrgenwerth will not appear.

            To summon him for The Shadow of Yharnam fight you must also have the Impurity Rune equipped, and to have not killed Valtyr.


            Hypogean Gaol            		 Defector Antal: Summon sign glows a red hue.
            His summon is located in Hypogean Gaol  before Darkbeast Paarl after dropping down from the ledge on the right hand side.

            His summon sign is located just before The One Reborn boss fight hidden by the overturned cart.

            He wields the Church Pick with Flamesprayer while wearing the Yahar'gul Set.

            Old Hunter Yamamura can be summoned for Darkbeast Paarl and the One Reborn. He wields the Chikage and the Piercing Rifle and wears the Yamamura Set.


            Great Isz Chalice            		 Old Hunter Madman Wallar: Summon sign glows a indigo hue.
            His summon sign is located in the room with the wolf after the rolling rock on your right.

            He wields the Amygdalan Arm with Ludwig's Rifle while wearing the Madman attire.
            Old Hunter Tomb Prospector Gremia: Summon sign glows a red hue.

            Her summon sign is located behind the supply cart near the boss gate.

            She wields the Ludwig holy sword and Torch/Rosmarinus while wearing the tomb prospector attire.


            Great Ihyll Chalice            		 Old Hunter Madman Wallar: Summon sign glows a indigo hue.

            His summon sign is located behind a rock near the hostile Tomb Prospector Npc.

            He wields the Amygdalan Arm with Ludwig's Rifle while wearing the Madman attire.
            Old Hunter Tomb Prospector Olek: Summon sign glows a red hue.

            His summon sign is located on your left after leaving the lamp near the beginning of the level.

            He wields the Kirkhammer and torch/repeating pistol while wearing the Tomb Prospector attire.

            Impurity Rune ISN'T needed to summon both; Can summon both for boss fight.
            		Old Hunter Father Gascoigne: Summon sign glows a red hue.

            His summon sign is located near the fountain that is near the laughing house/the first Brick Troll you encounter in Central Yharnam.

            He wields the Hunter Axe and Hunter Pistol while wearing his signature attire set.


            Ailing Loran Chalice            		 Old Hunter Vitus: Summon sign glows a red hue.
            His sign is located before the boss fight on layer 3.
            Wearing dlc attire and using a hunter pistol with the beast club.
            Old Hunter Beastclaw Jozef: Summon sign glows a red hue. It is also located on layer 3, taking the passage to the right after leaving the lamp room, before entering the big room with the madman. Beware of spiders.
            wearing charred attire with beast claw.
            Summoning one cancels the other.


            Lower Loran            		 Beastclaw Jozel & Old Hunter Vittus on Layer 3, Vitus can be summoned in the first room after the lamp, on the left side, Jozel can be summoned in the middle of a circular stair room at the bottom.
            Hunter's Nigtmare

            Old Hunter Valtr without helmet for the Ludwig boss fight. He can also be summoned for the Laurence boss fight. His sign can be found right after entering the building he is in.

            Old Hunter Henriett can be summoned from down the stairs near the Nightmare Church (the second) lantern.
            Note: She does not help much with the fight so it is recommended to summon Valtr instead.

            Younger Madaras Twin can be summoned in the Hunter's Nightmare just before Ludwig, where the axe wielding Bell Holder enemy walks out from.
            Central Pthumeru Layer 3 Olek red sign dont need run to summon.
