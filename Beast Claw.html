            The Beast Claw is a Trick Weapon in Bloodborne it's associated with The Blood Beast Covenant.

             

            Beast Claw Description

            "Beastly weapon wielded by Irreverent Izzy.
            Crafted by chiseling the long bones of an undead darkbeast and fastening them to the weapon. The bones are still alive, and when unleashed, grant its wielder a spurt of beastly power.
            As flesh is flayed and blood is sprayed, the beast within awakens, and in time, the wielder of this weapon surges with both strength and feverish reverie."

             

            Beast Claw Mode Information

            • The regular form is a simple claw weapon that is used like an extended fist.
            • The transformed mode morphs the Hunter's arms into a werewolf form.
            • If you have the Beast's Embrace rune equipped, this weapon will gain additional attacks and change how the Hunter fights.
              • For more information, check the Beast's Embrace rune page on the new effects it has on the Beast Claw.

             

             

            Location

            Normal

            • Ailing Loran Chalice: Layer 2 treasure room. Room is on the right when exiting the elevator from first layer boss fight, before the second layers lantern.
            • Can be bought from messengers for 34000 echoes after acquisition.
            • Can be bought from messengers after co-operation with player who has them. This only occurs in Hunter's dream before fight with Gehrman or Moon Presence. It is not needed to complete any Chalice Dungeons. It is unknown if this is glitch or intentional. Was patched by 1.07 or 1.08.

            Uncanny Beast Claw Mode

            • Ailing Loran Root Chalice
            • Glyphs: wxa3twxp (Layer 2)
            • Ailing Loran Root Chalice
            • Glyph: xmmfy2mk (Layer 1, first door on your left after spawning in the Chamber of the Seal, much easier to get for low level hunters)

            Lost Beast Claw Mode

            • Lower Loran Root Chalice
            • Sinister Lower Loran Root Chalice
            • Glyph: 7r2q7gs5 (Layer 3)
            • Lower Loran Root Chalice (Cursed/Fetid/Rotten)
            • Glyph: xeqqu3t7 (Layer 1, At the end of first side area on the right from Chamber of the Seal) (There's also a Blood Rock on layer 2)

             

             

            Player Notes

            • The regular form of this weapon acts like a simple claw weapon.
              • It's useful for quick attacks against an opponent when you aren't quite ready for the transformed claw's Beasthood mode.
                • However, keep in mind this weapon has a slightly faster attack speed when transformed.
                • Learn when to strike the opponent and unleash the full fury of the Beast Claw while utilizing the increased damage dealt from Beasthood.
              • Remember that you can also use a Firearm in this mode.
              • Your ability to Rally is slightly higher in regular form compared to the transformed mode.
            • When transformed, the true power of the Beast Claw is unleashed.
              • The attacks in this stance will create a perpetual Beast Blood Pellet effect.
                • If you use an actual Beast Blood Pellet, it will not change anything in regards to the Beasthood generated by this weapon.
                • In fact, it resets the Beasthood meter when the pill's effect wears off, which can potentially be harmful if you depended on the increased damage inflicted on opponents.
              • Transforming back into the regular form will not reset the Beasthood meter.
                • Switching to a different weapon will not reset the meter as well.
                • Due to this fact, you can use the transformed claws to build up the Beasthood meter, then switch to a different weapon and enjoy its effects while it lasts.
            • The fast attacks of this weapon make it ideal for poison blood gems.
            • It can be buffed with Fire Paper, Bolt Paper and the Empty Phantasm Shell.
              • You can also modify its damage type with blood gems.
              • However, because non-physical damage isn't affected by Beasthood, while still incurring the defense penalty, high arcane builds aren't very effective with this weapon.
            • When facing mobs of enemies, use your superior attack speed to effectively hit and run.
              • The Beast Claw does have some multi-attacks, but in general it's not a weapon suited for fighting mobs.
              • Keep in mind it can kill enemies very quickly if you have high Beasthood.

            Trivia

            • When this weapon is equipped, your gloves will be replaced by two bone gauntlets. This can allow for the lost art of rapid glove changing if you switch your weapons really fast.
            • You can charge the beasthood meter on the first windows (Gilbert) of Central Yharnam
            • In the past, there was a rare glitch that allowed a player to obtain this weapon without finding it in the Chalice Dungeons.
              • It has since been patched, although it isn't quite known when it was successfully patched.

                                                                                                                                                                                                          The reason the Beast Claw is categorized as a Chruch weapon is because it was designed by Irreverent Izzy, who was affiliated with the Healing Church workshop. She's a bit akin to Archibald in that they both were hunters who tried to innovate and change the old ways of creating trick weapons, and that they both relied on the Darkbeast as a reference point for their creations. Izzy, however, went a bit overboard in my opinion, her creations being too closely associated with Beasthood for any sane hunter to use.
