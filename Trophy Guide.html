            Guides & Walkthroughs
              +

            Trophies in Bloodborne are unlockable digital shelf rewards for your in-game accomplishments. This page lists the total trophies and a guide on how to obtain them. Please beware of spoilers.

            • Click the table header to sort.

             

            Bloodborne Trophies

            Trophy How to Obtain Value

            Bloodborne

            All trophies acquired. Hats off!            		 Get every other trophy.

            Yharnam Sunrise

            You lived through the hunt, and saw another day.            		 Accept Gehrman's final request.

            Honouring Wishes

            Captivated by the moon presence, you pledge to watch over the hunter's dream.            		 Defeat Gehrman without ever consuming three One Third of Umbilical Cord.

            Childhood's Beginning

            You became an infant Great One, lifting humanity into its next childhood.            		 Acquire and consume three One Third of Umbilical Cord. Before defeating Gehrman.

            Yharnam, Pthumerian Queen

            Defeat Yharnam, Blood Queen of the Old Labyrinth.            		 Defeat Yharnam, a boss at the final layer of the Great Pthumeru Ihyll Chalice.

            Hunter's Essence

            Acquire all hunter weapons            		 Acquire all Weapons. Firearms included.

            Hunter's Craft

            Acquire all special hunter tools.            		 Acquire all Hunter Tools.

            Weapon Master

            Acquire a weapon of the highest level.            		 Fully upgrade a Weapon. See Workshop for information on how to do this.

            Blood Gem Master

            Acquire an extremely precious blood gem.            		 Obtain a Blood Gem of rank 15 or higher which can be found in depth 4+ Dungeons.

            Rune Master

            Acquire an extremely precious Caryll Rune.            		 In the elevator behind the locked right door in Cathedral Ward there's a hidden path that leads to one of these runes.

            Cainhurst

            Gain entry to Cainhurst, the lost and ruined castle.            		 Acquire Cainhurst Summons, then head to the monument at Hemwick's Crossing.

            The Choir

            Gain entry to the realm of the Choir, the high stratum of the Healing Church.            		 Acquire Upper Cathedral Key, and head to the top of the Cathedral Ward.

            The Source of the Dream

            Discover the abandoned old workshop, the source of the hunter's dream.            		 Hidden door in the cathedral towers basement. Don't jump all the way down.

            Nightmare Lecture Building

            Gain entry into the Byrgenwerth lecture building, that drifts within the realm of nightmare.            		 After defeating The One Reborn or being grabbed by the lesser Amygdala in Cathedral Ward while having the tonsil stone in your inventory.

            Father Gascoigne

            Defeat the beast that once was Father Gascoigne.            		 Defeat Father Gascoigne in Central Yharnam.

            Vicar Amelia

            Defeat the beast that once was Vicar Amelia.            		 Defeat Vicar Amelia in Cathedral Ward.

            Shadow of Yharnam

            Defeat the Shadow of Yharnam.            		 Defeat Shadow of Yharnam in Forbidden Woods.

            Rom, the Vacuous Spider

            Defeat Great One: Rom, the Vacuous Spider.            		 Defeat Rom, the Vacuous Spider at Moonside Lake.

            The One Reborn

            Defeat the One Reborn.            		 Defeat The One Reborn in Yahar'gul Chapel.

            Micolash, Host of the Nightmare

            Defeat Micolash, Host of the Nightmare.            		 Defeat Micolash, Host of the Nightmare in Mergo's Loft Base.

            Mergo's Wet Nurse

            Defeat Great One: Mergo's Wet Nurse.            		 Defeat Mergo's Wet Nurse in Mergo's Loft: Middle.

            Cleric Beast

            Defeat Cleric Beast.            		 Defeat Cleric Beast in Central Yharnam.

            Blood-starved Beast

            Defeat Blood-starved Beast.            		 Defeat Blood-starved Beast in Old Yharnam.

            The Witch of Hemwick

            Defeat the Witch of Hemwick.            		 Defeat Witch of Hemwick in Hemwick Charnel Lane.

            Darkbeast Paarl

            Defeat Darkbeast Paarl.            		 Defeat Darkbeast Paarl in Graveyard of the Darkbeast.

            Amygdala

            Defeat Great One: Amygdala.            		 Defeat Amygdala in Nightmare Frontier.

            Martyr Logarius

            Defeat Martyr Logarius.            		 Defeat Martyr Logaruis in Cainhurst Castle.

            Celestial Emissary

            Defeat Great One: Celestial Emissary.            		 Defeat Celestial Emissary in Lunar Flower Garden.

            Ebrietas, Daughter of the Cosmos

            Defeat Great One: Ebrietas, Daughter of the Cosmos.            		 Defeat Ebrietas, Daughter of the Cosmos in the Altar of Despair.

            Blood Gem Contact

            Acquire a blood gem that imbues hunter weapons with special strength            		 Acquire a Blood Gem.

            Rune Contact

            Acquire a Caryll Rune that endows hunters with special strength.            		 Acquire a Caryll Rune.

            Chalice of Pthumeru

            Acquire the Chalice of Pthumeru that seals the catacombs that form a web deep below Yharn...            		 Acquire Chalice of Pthumeru (defeat Blood-Starved Beast).

            Chalice of Ailing Loran

            Acquire the Chalice of Ailing Loran that seals the tragic land lost to the sands.            		 Defeat Amygdala.

            Chalice of Isz

            Acquire the Great Chalice of Isz that seals the home of the cosmic kin.            		 Defeat Ebrietas, Daughter of the Cosmos.



            The Old Hunters Trophies

             

            Old Hunter's Essence

            Acquire all old hunter weapons.            		 Acquire all The Old Hunters Weapons

            Orphan of Kos

            Defeat Great One: Orphan of Kos.            		 Defeat Orphan of Kos

            Ludwig, the Holy Blade

            Defeat the beast that was once Ludwig, the Holy Blade.            		 Defeat Ludwig

            Lady Maria of the Astral Clocktower

            Defeat Lady Maria of the Astral Clocktower.            		 Defeat Lady Maria

            Living Failures

            Defeat the failed attempts to become Great Ones.            		 Defeat Living Failures

            Laurence, the First Vicar

            Defeat the beast that was once Laurence, the First Vicar.            		 Defeat Laurence

                It's possible to get all three endings without New Game+. You need a thumb drive and save data that hasn't eaten 3 umbilical cords and is right before Gehrman. Transfer the save data to the drive, load the game, accept Gehrman's offer. Once you get the trophy, quit, and transfer the save on the drive to your system. Load the game, Decline the offer, kill Gerhman, get the trophy, quit. Transfer the save to the system, nom the cords, kill Gehrman, kill Moon Presence, get trophy. Ta-da.
