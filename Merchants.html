            World Information / NPCs
              +

            Merchants in Bloodborne are special NPCs that interact with the player to exchange currency or items for other items. The player will find these vendors during regular gameplay, they are all different kinds of Messengers.

             

            Bath Messengers Merchant

            "Trade in blood echoes with messengers to buy and sell items."

            The Bath Merchant can be found at the beginning of the game in the Hunters Dream. Players can buy and sell items here with blood echoes.

             

            Bath Messengers Location:

            Hunters Dream - just to the left of where you wake up at the start of the game.
