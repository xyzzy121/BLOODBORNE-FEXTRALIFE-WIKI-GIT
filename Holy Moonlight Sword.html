            Holy Moonlight Sword is a Trick Weapon in Bloodborne. It was introduced with The Old Hunters DLC.
            There are 3 versions of this weapon: Normal, Uncanny, and Lost. The only known differences being the Gem Imprints and the locations in which they are found.

             

            Holy Moonlight Sword Description

            "An arcane sword discovered long ago by Ludwig.
            When blue moonlight dances around the sword, and it channels the abyssal cosmos, its great blade will hurl a shadowy lightwave.
            The Holy Moonlight Sword is synonymous with Ludwig, the Holy Blade, but few have ever set eyes on the great blade, and whatever guidance it has to offer, it seems to be of a very private, elusive sort."

             

            Holy Moonlight Sword Information

            • Transforms from a one-handed Greatsword into the two-handed Moonlight Greatsword.
            • 50% righteous bonus for all attacks.
            • Untransformed R1 attacks deal 50% of the listed arcane damage.
              • Untransformed -> Transformed L1 deals 83.3% of the listed arcane damage.
            • Transformed R1 attacks deal 70% of the listed arcane damage.
              • Transformed -> Untransformed L1 deals 100% of the listed arcane damage.
            • Transformed R2 attacks (with bullets) deal 0% of the listed arcane damage; 100% of the arcane damage is transferred to the sword's projectile.
            • Transformed  R2 attacks (without bullets) deal 70% of the listed arcane damage, as there is no projectile.
            • The L2 attack (with bullets) deals 100% of the listed arcane damage.
            • The L2 attack (without bullets) deals 70% of the listed arcane damage.
            • The Normal variant performs well enough, allowing for triangular gems such as Odd (Temp/Arc) or Abyssal Nourishing gems.
            • The Uncanny variant performs slightly worse, allowing only Abyssal Nourishing gems, as fire and bolt are wasted on this weapon.
            • The Lost variant performs best due to its triangle and waning gem slots, which can be utilized for two strong Abyssal Nourishing gems.

             

             

            Location

            Normal

            • The Hunter's Nightmare: Found on the Underground Corpse Pile after the fight against Ludwig, The Holy Blade. Ludwig's head will be lying there and you can:
              • Kill the head for the sword.
              • Wear a piece of any church attire and talk to the head. When prompted, reply "Yes" and you'll get the sword.
              • Wear a piece of any church attire and reply "No", then come back later and Simon the Harrowed will be there and give you the sword.
              • NOTE: This weapon is missable. If Laurence is killed before you obtain it, you will no longer be able to talk to the head or obtain it from Simon.

            Uncanny Holy Moonlight Sword

            • If you have the normal version: Create Glyph 8i29rqz3 (Pthumeru Labyrinth) and buy from Bath Messengers up a ladder from the main room for 80,000 Blood Echoes.

            Lost Holy Moonlight Sword

            • If you have the normal version: Create Glyph 8i29rqz3 (Pthumeru Labyrinth) and buy from Bath Messengers up a ladder from the main room for 80,000 Blood Echoes.

             

             

            Player Notes

            • All arcane damage %s (presented in the weapon's information above) are applied to the individual attacks' multipliers. E.G., the transformed running R1 has a 1.1 multiplier, so its arcane damage multiplier is 0.7 * 1.1 = 0.77. Thus, if your Holy Moonlight Sword's stats read that it has "100 + 50" arcane damage, you will deal (100 + 50) * 0.77 = 115.5 arcane damage.
            • Transformed R2 attacks at close range will hit foes with both the sword and the projectile, dealing 100% of the physical and arcane damage listed.
            • Without QS bullets, the transformed heavy attacks will no longer emit projectiles.
            • Cannot be buffed with consumables such as Empty Phantasm Shell, Bolt Paper, or Fire Paper.
            • Cannot be changed to a different element by using Gems that give Fire or Bolt bonuses.
            • The weapon has poise / hyper-armor frames in both of its normal and transformed modes. You cannot be interrupted by light attacks mid-swing while you are attacking an enemy.

            Trivia

            • It seems to be another reappearance of the Moonlight Greatsword, a recurring item found around FromSoftware's games. Notable examples of such games include the first, second and third Dark Souls games, Demon's Souls, King's Field, Ninja Blade, Enchanted Arms, Armored Core, 3D Dot Game Heroes, and Otogi.
