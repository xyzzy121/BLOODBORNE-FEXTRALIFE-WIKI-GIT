            General Information
              +

            DLC or downloadable content is being released in the form of The Old Hunters expansion, due to be released November 24th worldwide. A new hard copy bundle of the original game along with the expansion will be released a week later.
            For pre-release, we are only aware of special Messengers skins that the player may obtain via special editions or preorder bonuses. You can find more information here .

            Bloodborne Expansion


            The Old Hunters DLC trailer revealed!

            • Releasing in Japan November 2015 - A re-release including the expansion will also be available from December 3rd
            • New Weapons such as a bow, new bosses, and possibly new Hunter Tools
            • See the The Old Hunters page for trailer
            • (legacy): An expansion has been revealed, with more news coming later in the year Read here

            So I have been asked by many people if we are making DLC for Bloodborne. I can say... An expansion is coming. More info later this year. :D

            — Shuhei Yoshida (@yosp) May 21, 2015


            Celebration DLC

            • To commemorate sales of 1,000,000 Bloodborne copies, a Messenger's Hat skin will be released on Playstation store on April 16th

            おかげさまで『Bloodborne(ブラッドボーン)』は全世界累計実売本数100万本を突破しました。感謝を込めて「使者の包帯頭」を4/16よりPS Storeにて無料配信します。お楽しみに pic.twitter.com/U5VYtdI6WF

            — 山際眞晃Masaaki Yamagiwa (@giwamasa) April 14, 2015


            Pre-Release DLC

            • Messenger Skins at selected shops such as Gamestop and via pre-order bonuses
            • PS4 Theme for Uk and EU
