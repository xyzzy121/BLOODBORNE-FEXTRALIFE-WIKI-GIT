            Ritual Blood

            Material used in a Holy Chalice rite.

            One of the basic ingredients used to satiate a Holy Chalice is this incoagulable blood.

            When all is melted in blood, all is reborn.

            Ritual Blood is an item in Bloodborne that can be combined with other Ritual Materials to create a Chalice Dungeon.

             

            Ritual Blood Usage

            • Used to create a Chalice Dungeon

             

             

            Location

            • Can be purchased from Messengers
            • Can be farmed in various Chalice Dungeons

             

             

            Notes

            • ??

             

            Lore

            • "When all is melted in blood, all is reborn" might describe the Mensis Ritual and The One Reborn.

            Ratings


            Ritual Blood (1)            		 Used in Chalices: Pthumeru Chalice, Pthumeru Root Chalice
            • x2 found on altar in building with hanging beast remains in Old Yharnam.
            • Can be purchased for 2500 Blood Echoes each from the Messengers in Hunter's Dream after defeating Blood-starved Beast.
            • Can be found inside Depth 1 Chalice Dungeons.

            Ritual Blood (2)            		 Used in Chalices: Central Pthumeru Chalice, Central Pthumeru Root Chalice, Hintertomb Chalice, Hintertomb Root Chalice
            • Can be purchased for 6000 Blood Echoes each from messengers in Hunter's Dream (after completing Lower Pthumeru Chalice) or from messengers inside Chalice Dungeons
            • Can be found inside Depth 1 and Depth 2 Chalice Dungeons.

            Ritual Blood (3)            		 Used in Chalices: Lower Pthumeru Chalice, Lower Pthumeru Root Chalice, Lower Hintertomb Chalice, Lower Hintertomb Root Chalice, Sinister Hintertomb Root Chalice
            • Can be found inside Depth 2 and Depth 3 Chalice Dungeons.
            • Can be purchased for 12000 Blood Echoes each from messengers in Hunter's Dream after obtaining the Great Pthumeru Ihyll Chalice (by completing Defiled Chalice)

            Ritual Blood (4)            		 Used in Chalices: Defiled Chalice, Cursed and Defiled Root Chalice, Ailing Loran Chalice, Ailing Loran Root Chalice
            • Can be found inside Depth 3 and Depth 4 Chalice Dungeons.
            • Can be purchased for 24000 Blood Echoes each from messengers in Hunter's Dream after obtaining Yharnam Stone (by completing Great Pthumeru Ihyll Chalice)
            • Farming spot: From the layer 2 lamp in the Lower Pthumeru Chalice, after unlocking the gate, go into the right side door in the hallway. Go forward and take the ladder up by the scorpion, To your left is a stairway with a Snatcher, and another at the top of those stairs. They both have a high chance of dropping.
            • Farming spot: Drops from Merciless Watcher  in Lower Pthumeru Chalice. From the Layer 2 Lantern hop down the pit and continue through the archway, through the tunnel, turn right, climb the ladder, turn left. Continue across both bridges. In the next room is a Merciless Watcher that drops them.
            • Farming spot: Drops from Snatcher in Lower Pthumeru Chalice Glyph: pe5imqwh. 1st layer 2 Snatchers kill them and hunter mark to repeat until you have enough Ritual Blood. (posted on reddit by user jdfred06)

            Ritual Blood (5)            		 Used in Chalices: Great Isz Chalice, Pthumeru Ihyll Root Chalice, Sinister Pthumeru Ihyll Root Chalice, Lower Loran Chalice, Lower Ailing Loran Root Chalice, Sinister Lower Loran Root Chalice, Great Isz Chalice, Isz Root Chalice, Sinister Isz Root Chalice
            • x3 found in front of the gate where you need the Orphanage Key in Upper Cathedral Ward.
            • Can be found inside Depth 4 Chalice Dungeons.
            • Bath Messengers in NG+ for 48.000 Blood Echoes
            • Easy farming spot: Glyph: crd4ss4s. In the first room after the Layer 1 lamp, there are 3 chests, 1 hidden in the smoke to the left. There is a total of 4 Ritual Blood (5)'s in these chests. Remake the chalice to farm.
            • Easy farming spot: Glyph: cxsee9xf. From the Layer 1 lamp, take a right into the side room. There will be a chest directly to your left with 2x Ritual Blood (5). Run back to the lamp and remake for more.
            • Easy farming spot: From the Layer One lamp in Ailing Loran Chalice go right in the big room, behind the ladder there is a tunnel that leads to a room with a Werewolf and a small chest with x2 Ritual Blood (5). Hunter's Mark out and search for another online dungeon. Repeat (You can do this unlimited)
            • Offline farming spot: From the first lamp in Defiled Chalice, turn right in the big hall and enter the tunnel at the opposite end, then take another right turn to get to a room with a lever and a respawning Merciless Watcher who will drop the item. The Ritual Master in the big hall will also drop Ritual Blood (4).
